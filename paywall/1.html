PAYWALL
Issue #1

The first issue of PAYWALL includes Paid Column in its entirety by Aurora San Miguel, covering the first year of exhibitions at Paid and includes previously unpublished reviews. It also includes contributions from Aaron Flint Jamison, Charles Stobbs III, Divya Mehra, Elizabeth VR, and Jason Hirata. It also includes more. This issue is comprised of four folded pages of inkjet printed text on 13" x 19" paper and one laser printed unfolded 8.5" x 11" sheet of paper. Each larger paper includes wood block printing and are folded by hand.

This issue is available in an edition of 50.
