PAYWALL
Issue #1

The first issue of PAYWALL includes Paid Column in its entirety by Aurora San Miguel, covering the first year of exhibitions at Paid and includes previously unpublished reviews. It also includes contributions from Aaron Flint Jamison, Charles Stobbs III, Divya Mehra, Elizabeth VR, and Jason Hirata. It also includes more. This issue consists of four folded pages of inkjet printed text and images on 13" x 19" paper and one laser printed unfolded 8.5" x 11" sheet of paper. There is also wood block printing and is folded by hand.

This issue is available in an edition of 50.
