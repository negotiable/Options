Paywall No. 2

The second issue of Paywall includes pull quotes from interviews which took place on the occasion of exhibitions following the second year of programming at Paid, conducted and transcribed by Aurora San Miguel.

Exhibitions at Paid between January 2023 and October 2023: Nicholas Strobelt, Kieran Daly, Flint Jamison, Charles Stobbs III & Neo Gibson, Park McArthur, Julia Yerger & Wiley Guillot, and Sean Lockwood.

It includes less than the first issue of Paywall.

This issue contains six unbound color laser printed A3 pages, one printed transparency, cover sheet, and is folded by hand.

Printing by Device for Paid

This issue is available in an edition of 50.
